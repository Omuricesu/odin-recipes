Ramen

Description

A Japanese noodle soup, with a combination of a rich flavoured broth, one of a variety of types of noodle and a selection of meats or vegetables, often topped with a boiled egg

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Ramen Broth
  • Nori (Japanese Seaweed)
  • Soy sauce
  • Ramen Egg

Steps

  1. Boil Ramen Noodles until soft
  2. Heat up Ramen Broth
  3. Put Ramen Broth, Soy Sauce, Ramen Noodles in bowl
  4. Top with Nori and Ramen Egg
