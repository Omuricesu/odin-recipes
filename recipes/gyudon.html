Gyudon

Description

A Japanese dish of thinly sliced beef and onions served over rice in a sweet and savory sauce. The name translates to "beef bowl"

Ingredients

  • Thinly Sliced Beef
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Dashi (Japanese Stock)

Steps

  1. Slice Onions
  2. Saute Onions and beef in pan
  3. Add beef stock and sauce soy
  4. Simmer and then serve over rice
