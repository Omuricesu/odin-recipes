Sushi Recipe

Description

A Japanese dish of vinegared rice with various toppings, including raw or cooked seafood. The rice is the main component of sushi, not the fish.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Toppings
    • Avocado
    • Salmon
    • Cucumber
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Shape Sushi Rice into a small nugget, approximately in palm
  2. Place Toppings on top of rice
  3. Add small teaspoon soy sauce on top
